Technical Pillars

System
Architecture

Four interlocking modules that form the BIOLOOP closed-loop pipeline — from raw CO₂ intake through biological conversion, downstream processing, and real-time computational oversight.

01

Carbon Capture Module

Industrial and distributed capture interface. Post-combustion CO₂ intake from flue gas, urban ambient capture nodes, and photobioreactor feed integration.

Input Layer
02

Biological Conversion

High-lipid algae strains in controlled photobioreactors. Bidirectional mixing system, strain optimization, and continuous cultivation protocols for maximum lipid yield.

Core Process
03

Extraction & Processing

Lipid extraction pipeline, transesterification for biodiesel, and Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) pathway adaptation. Biomass residue recovery and valorization loops.

Output Layer
04

Computational Layer

Net CO₂ impact model, EROI calculator, process optimization engine, carbon credit quantification logic, and Edge AI bioaerosol monitoring for real-time reactor control.

Conference Poster · AAC 2025

Real-Time Monitoring of Bioaerosol Emissions in Algae-Based Hydrogen Reactors Using Low-Cost Sensors and AI

A non-invasive "aerosol fingerprinting" framework using low-cost sensors and Edge-AI to analyze reactor headspace emissions. 94.6% classification accuracy (P=0.91, R=0.88) with 3–4 hour early warning advantage over optical methods.

Prakul Sunil Hiremath · VTU Belagavi · Mumbai, Dec 2025
