About
AoE

Aliens on Earth is an independent research collective working at the collision of disciplines — mathematics meets energy engineering, computation meets medicine, theory meets prototype.

Founding Message

Why We
Exist

AoE was founded with a single conviction — that the most important problems of our time demand researchers who think differently. Not differently for novelty's sake, but because the old frameworks are insufficient.

We call ourselves Aliens on Earth because we believe the best science feels alien until it becomes obvious. Every project we pursue has a pathway to deployment. Research that stays in a paper is incomplete research.

Vision

Where We're
Going

A future where clean energy is abundant, computational medicine is precise, and mathematical foundations power real-world systems — built by researchers unbound by institutional inertia.

Aligned with India's National Hydrogen Mission, National Biofuel Mission, and the clean energy mandate for 2030. Our research translates into startups, grants, and government-backed pilots.
