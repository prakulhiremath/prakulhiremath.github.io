AoE operates as a founder-led independent collective. No departmental silos. No institutional bureaucracy. Researchers move across all three pillars as projects demand.

Every member contributes to at least two of our three research domains. The computational foundations pillar is owned collectively — it belongs to everyone and serves everything.
